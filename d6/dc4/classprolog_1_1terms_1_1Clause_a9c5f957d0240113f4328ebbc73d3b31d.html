Algebraic composition operation of 2 Clauses, doing the basic resolution step Prolog is based on.

From A0:-A1,A2...An and B0:-B1...Bm it builds (A0:-B1,..Bm,A2,...An) mgu(A1,B0). Note that it returns null if A1 and B0 do not unify.
