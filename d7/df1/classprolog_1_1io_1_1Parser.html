Detailed Description

Simplified Prolog parser: Synatax supported: a0:-a1,...,an.

  • no operators ( except toplevel :- and ,)
  • use quotes to create special symbol names, i.e. compute('+',1,2, Result) and write(':-'(a,','(b,c)))
